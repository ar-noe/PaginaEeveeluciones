FLAREON

Flareon (Japanese: ブースター, Hepburn: Buusutaa) (FLAIR-ee-on[1]) is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is one of the eight final forms of Eevee, evolved by using a Fire Stone.

Flareon is a quadrupedal, mammalian, feliform Pokémon. It has vivid flame-orange fur with a bushy yellow mane, a wide, fluffy flame-shaped long tail, and a tuft of yellow fur atop its head, also flame-like in shape. This Pokémon has a short cat-like muzzle, wide black almond-shaped eyes, and a small black triangular nose. Its ears are long and rabbit-like, with flare-like tips. It has large, bulky paws with no visible claws. Flareon's body when not including the fluffy hair is generally bulkier than most of the Eeveelutions but is still slightly smaller than Vaporeon. Of all the Eeveelutions, Flareon resembles its pre-evolution the most.

When Shiny, Flareon's red-orange fur is a lighter, yellow-gold shade of color.

Type:

Fire

Habilities :

  • Flash Fire
  • Guts

Evolves From: Eevee

Flareon's fluffy fur releases heat into the air so that its body does not get excessively hot. Its body temperature can rise to a maximum of 1,650 degrees F.
