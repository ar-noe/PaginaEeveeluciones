ESPEON

Espeon (Japanese: エーフィ, Hepburn: Eefi) (ESS-pee-on[1]) is a psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It is one of the eight final forms of Eevee, an evolution resulting from leveling up while having high friendship during the daytime. It is also the player's First partner Pokémon alongside Umbreon in Pokémon Colosseum.

Espeon is a quadruped mammalian Pokémon with cat-like features. It has sleek and lilac purple fur covers its body. It has a red jewel attached to its forehead. This Pokémon's face is distinctly cat-like with a short, blunt muzzle and a tiny, triangular nose. It has long pointed ears, and whisker-like tufts of fur protruding from each side of its face just below its ears. Its eyes are deep purple and almond-shaped with white pupils. It has a slender tail that tapers and splits into two ends. Of all the Eeveelutions, Espeon resembles a small feline (such as a domestic cat) the most.

As a Shiny, Espeon has bright green fur while the insides of its ears turn blue. Its eyes and gem remain unchanged.

Type:

Psychic

Habilities :

  • Synchronize
  • Magic Bounce

Evolves From: Eevee

An Espeon is loyal to any Trainer it considers to be worthy. It is said to have developed precognitive powers to protect its Trainer from harm.
