GLACEON

Glaceon (Japanese: グレイシア, Hepburn: Gureishia) (GLACE-ee-on[1]) is an Ice-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV and is one of the eight evolutions forms of Eevee.

Glaceon is a quadruped mammalian Pokémon that bears a resemblance to an Arctic animal. Its fur is a light-blue color, with diamond-shaped patterns on its back, tail, and ears in a darker shade of blue. Glaceon has a cerulean blue cap-like feature on its forehead with two long blue strips dangling from either side. It has a short feline muzzle with a small triangular nose along with long pointed rabbit-like ears. Glaceon's tail is long with a pointed dark blue diamond-shaped tip. It has small dainty paws and feet that are dark blue in color.

Glaceon's Shiny variation appears to have lighter fur, its diamond patterns being a slightly darker blue. Its cap-like feature is sky blue.

Type:

Ice

Habilities :

  • Snow Cloak
  • Ice Body

Evolves From: Eevee

As a protective technique, it can completely freeze its fur to make its hairs stand like needles.
