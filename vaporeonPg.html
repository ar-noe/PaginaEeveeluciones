VAPOREON

Vaporeon (Japanese: シャワーズ, Hepburn: Shawaazu) (vay-POUR-ree-on[1]) is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is one of the eight final forms of Eevee, obtained by using a Water Stone.

Vaporeon shares physical traits with aquatic animals and feliforms in appearance. Its body is light-blue with a white, finned ruff around its neck. Vaporeon has a round head, a blunt, short, cat-like muzzle, and a small triangular nose. It has cream-yellowish ear-like fins on both sides of its head, along with a third fin-like appendage on the top of its forehead head. It has blue ridges that run down the length of its head, spine, and along its tail-fin. Its long tail resembles that of a dolphin, whale, or mermaid.

A Shiny Vaporeon is mostly light purple in color while its ridges turn dark purple. Its ear-like fins and ruff remain the same.

Type:

Water

Habilities :

  • Water Absorb
  • Hydrate

Evolves From: Eevee

Vaporeon underwent a spontaneous mutation and grew fins and gills that allow them to live underwater. They have the ability to freely control water.
